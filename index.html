WELCOME TO MEERUT COLLEGE

Meerut College, Meerut was started in 1892. It is a prestigious institution of Northern India and is catering to the higher education needs of both urban and rural areas. It has rightly been said GETTING ADMISSION IN MEERUT COLLEGE IS A MATTER OF PROUD. Every year thousands of students strive hard for admission in this prestigious institution of India. In the session 2014-15 have 10330 students are enrolled for studies Meerut College, Meerut has a glorious history of achievements in academics and other fields. Personalities from Prime Minister, Governors, Ambassadors, Cabinet Ministers, Member's of Parliament, State Assemblies, Judges, innumerable Administrators IAS, IFS, IRS, National Poets, Advocates, Social Workers, Scientists, leading Industrialist and Educationists have been students of this college. The institution is an important land mark in Meerut, of which the city is proud of. The College is situated in an open and peaceful atmosphere, covering an area of 100 acres of land.
