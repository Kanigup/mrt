ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Students seeking admission in Meerut College need to register at the CCS University website as well as at the college with required details of Marksheet and other certificates (as applicable). The admissions are done strictly on the basis of Merit lists that are displayed on the Notice board also on the website of the college. The Govt. of Uttar Pradesh policies and the CCS University guidelines are followed in relation to reservation of various categories.
